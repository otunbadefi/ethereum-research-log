Rollup-Centric Ethereum

Ethereum is moving toward a rollup-centric model.

In this design, most transactions happen on Layer 2 networks called rollups.

Rollups process transactions off-chain and then publish compressed data to Ethereum.

Ethereum acts as the settlement and security layer.

This approach helps Ethereum scale without sacrificing decentralization.

The main tradeoff is fragmentation across multiple rollups. Users and liquidity become spread across different networks.

Current work in the ecosystem focuses on improving interoperability between rollups and reducing these friction points.
